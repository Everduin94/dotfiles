Ideas

- having `completion.list.selection.preselect = true` + `enter` preset makes us "gun-shy".
- `completion.list.selection.preselect = false` might make `enter` fine.
  - If it's still annoying just turn enter off.
- Then use `tab` for cycling? And something like ctrl+y or ctrl+l for copilot?
  - `tab` seems ideal because not everything needs to be completed. Some things are just references. Then tab acts as a fast way to get a word from the buffer.
  - With preselect = true. You can't tab to the first item. You're already on the first item (but it's not actually typed out).
